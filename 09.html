Electro

Electro on its own does not have any special effect, but it will allow Electro Elemental Reactions to occur. Certain mechanics in Inazuma, such as the Lightning Strike Probe, will respond to characters or monsters affected by Electro. Any source of Electro DMG, including Electro Crystals, will apply the Electro status. Getting struck by lightning or entering a body of water struck by lightning during a thunderstorm will also apply Electro.

Overloaded: Causes an explosion that deals AoE Pyro DMG with high knockback.
Hydro Electro-Charged: Deals Electro DMG and high Poise Damage over time to all nearby targets with the Wet status
Lunar-Charged: Creates a thundercloud that continuously deals Electro DMG to nearby enemies that are affected by both Hydro and Electro
Superconduct: Deals AoE Cryo DMG and reduces the Physical RES of enemies caught in the AoE by 40%.
Swirl: Deals AoE Electro DMG while spreading the Electro status to nearby enemies.
Crystallize: Creates an Electro shard. Picking it up creates an Electro Shield around the character.
Quicken: Applies a Quicken aura
Aggravate: Triggered when applying Electro to an entity with the Quicken aura. Adds Additive Base DMG Bonus to the attack that triggers this reaction.
Spread: Triggered when applying Dendro to an entity with the Quicken aura. Adds Additive Base DMG Bonus to the attack that triggers this reaction.
