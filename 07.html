Inazuma

Inazuma (Japanese: 稲妻 Inazuma) is one of the seven nations of Teyvat and one of the major regions in Genshin Impact.
It is an archipelagic region that worships Raiden Shogun, the Electro Archon and God of Eternity — who is also the leader of its governing body, the Inazuma Shogunate.
Inazuma is located approximately 4 km southeast from Liyue Harbor and closely neighbors the Dark Sea.
